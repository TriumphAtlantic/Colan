📊 How We Calculate This (Click to Expand)

Software Waste Formula:
Revenue × 0.10% = Direct license waste
Example: $250M × 0.001 = $250K/year in unused software

Process Waste Formula:
PMs × 7.5 hrs/week × 52 weeks × $100/hr = Process friction cost
Example: 100 PMs × 7.5 × 52 × $100 = $3.9M/year in wasted time

Why 7.5 hours per week?
Industry research shows PMs spend 5–10 hrs/week on avoidable friction: searching for documents, duplicate data entry, chasing status updates, fixing handoff errors. We use the midpoint (7.5 hrs) for conservative estimates.
